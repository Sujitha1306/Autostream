# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is an AI-powered video editing SaaS platform built for content creators.
It automates repetitive editing tasks like captions, cuts, transitions, and color grading
so creators can focus on content, not editing.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan — $29/month
- 10 videos per month
- Maximum resolution: 720p
- Auto-cut and silence removal
- Basic transitions library
- Email support (2-business-day response)
- 1 user seat
- 5 GB cloud storage

### Pro Plan — $79/month
- Unlimited videos per month
- Maximum resolution: 4K Ultra HD
- AI Captions (auto-generated, 95% accuracy)
- Advanced AI scene detection
- Brand kit (logos, fonts, colors)
- Priority 24/7 live chat support
- 5 user seats
- 100 GB cloud storage
- API access for custom integrations
- Analytics dashboard

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
AutoStream does not issue refunds after 7 days from the date of purchase.
Users are encouraged to fully test the product within the 7-day window.
If you cancel within 7 days, a full refund is processed within 3–5 business days.

### Customer Support
- Basic Plan: Email support with 2-business-day response time
- Pro Plan: 24/7 live chat and priority phone support

### Free Trial
AutoStream offers a 7-day free trial on the Pro plan. No credit card required.

### Plan Upgrades and Downgrades
Users can upgrade from Basic to Pro at any time. Billing is prorated for the remaining period.
Downgrading from Pro to Basic takes effect at the next billing cycle.

### Data and Privacy
AutoStream does not sell user data to third parties. All video content is encrypted at rest.
Users own their content 100%.

### Supported Platforms for Import
AutoStream supports direct import from: YouTube Studio, TikTok Creator Portal,
Instagram, Facebook Creator Studio, Dropbox, and Google Drive.
